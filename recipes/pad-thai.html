Pad Thai

Description

This is a description of the Pad Thai recipe.

Ingredients

  • Teleportation circle
  • Some Thai Baht

Steps

  1. You step into the teleportation circle, which teleports you to Bangkok, the capital of Thailand.
  2. You walk along the streets till you find a pad thai stall.
  3. In your most charming voice, hand over your Thai Baht and ask for some Pad Thai.
